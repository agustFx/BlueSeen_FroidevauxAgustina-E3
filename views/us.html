BlueSeen

We are an app that is aimed at the entire audience that needs kind and warm support in difficult times.

Our goal is to provide a space where the user can interact with himself and the emotions he feels in the present moment.

For that we have questionnaires, simple and dynamic games, creative areas and entertainment suggestions.

All with the purpose of identifying and exploring the user's emotions as he himself decides.
